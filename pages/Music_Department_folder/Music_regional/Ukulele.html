Virtual Ukulele

Play notes directly from your keyboard. Each row maps to one string, from top to bottom: G, C, E, A. Tap the printed keys or click the frets to pluck.

Press any mapped key to wake the instrument.
Strings

Number row is G. `Q` row is C. `A` row is E. `Z` row is A.

Range

Each string has eight frets, so you can sketch melodies instead of just open strings.
